üåç About This Project

This is a CNN-based regression model that predicts the percentage of land and ocean visible in NASA EPIC satellite images. The model was trained to estimate the actual geographic composition of Earth as seen from the EPIC satellite.

üìç How Ground Truth Works

The ground truth is computed from geographic coordinates, not from pixel analysis. This means:

  • ‚úì Accuracy is independent of cloud cover, lighting, or image quality
  • ‚úì Based on actual landmass distribution at the satellite's viewpoint
  • ‚úì Reliable regardless of atmospheric conditions
  • ‚úì Uses geographic data computed from latitude/longitude coordinates
